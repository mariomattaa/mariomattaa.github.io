UA Beans

Brand / Landing Page
Coffee Shop
Single-page Website
30-minute Quiz
University Project

UA Beans is a fictional café brand for Université Antonine. The task: design a marketing website for the café in under 30 minutes during a timed university quiz.

The page had to instantly feel like a real place: warm branding, featured drink highlight, menu categories (coffee, pastries, tea, sandwiches), and physical locations with opening hours. No “app flow,” just strong visual identity and clarity.

Full Landing Page

The full one-page site: top nav, hero with featured drink, “Latest Product” callout, menu categories, and store locations with map and hours.

Why this project matters

This wasn’t a long project with research and iterations — it was a 30 minute timed quiz. I still treated it like a real brand. The structure, palette, typography, and menu presentation all work together to sell “this place exists” even if it doesn’t (yet).

UA Beans shows I can work fast, build a believable brand look, present physical locations, and organize real-world info (prices, opening hours) in a way that feels trustworthy.
